About Us

The Modern Tech Club empowers students proficient in diverse technology niches to amplify their impact by sharing their unparalleled experiences with fellow enthusiasts!

We offer high school students a transformative opportunity to bolster their College Applications through meaningful volunteer engagements! Members gain access to exclusive alerts regarding volunteer opportunities, enabling them to leverage their tech expertise to make a substantial difference in their community!
